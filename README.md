# 🎓 AI-Powered Multi-Stream Placement & Career Readiness Portal

## 📌 Overview

The AI-Powered Multi-Stream Placement & Career Readiness Portal is a web-based platform designed to help students prepare for placements, interviews, and career growth across multiple streams.

The portal supports students from:

* IT & Software
* Accounting & Finance
* Banking & Insurance
* Human Resources
* Marketing
* Government Exam Preparation

---

## 🚀 Features

### 📄 Resume Analyzer

* Resume review and suggestions
* Career-specific recommendations
* PDF Resume Upload Support

### 🧠 Multi-Stream Quiz

* IT & Software Quiz
* Accounting & Finance Quiz
* Banking & Insurance Quiz
* Human Resources Quiz
* Marketing Quiz
* Government Exams Quiz

### 🎤 Interview Preparation

* HR Interview Questions
* Technical Interview Questions
* Domain-Specific Questions

### 📊 Career Guidance

* Career Roadmaps
* Skill Development Suggestions
* Career Recommendations

### 🏆 Certificate Generation

* Download Career Readiness Certificate
* PDF Certificate Generation

### 💾 Local Storage

* Stores student progress
* Saves quiz scores and recommendations
